Wake-Up Smoothie

Description

A refreshing and healthy smoothie to start your day! Packed with a variety of fruits and creamy yogurt, this smoothie is perfect for breakfast or as a mid-morning snack. The combination of strawberries, pineapples, mango, and grapes provides a deliciously sweet and tangy flavor, while the greek yogurt adds a creamy texture and a boost of protein. The orange juice and milk help to thin out the smoothie and make it easier to drink, while also adding a touch of citrusy brightness. Top with whipped cream for an extra indulgent treat!

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup frozen strawberries
  • 1/4 cup frozen pineapples
  • 1/4 cup frozen mango
  • 1/4 cup frozen grapes
  • 1/4 cup greek yogurt
  • 1/4 cup orange juice
  • 1/2 cup milk

Instructions

  1. Combine all ingredients in a blender.
  2. Blend until smooth and creamy.
  3. Top with whipped cream if desired.
  4. Pour into a glass and enjoy!
