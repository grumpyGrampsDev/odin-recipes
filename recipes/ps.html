Comfort Potato Soup

Description

A hearty and comforting potato soup that's perfect for chilly days! This soup is made with tender potatoes, rich evaporated milk, and a generous amount of butter, creating a creamy and satisfying base. The addition of shredded cheddar cheese adds a deliciously savory flavor, while the chopped bacon provides a smoky and crispy contrast. Finally, the chopped chives add a fresh and vibrant touch to the soup, making it a perfect meal for any day of the week.

Ingredients

  • 6 potatoes
  • 1 stick of butter
  • 1 can (12 oz) evaporated milk
  • 1/2 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1/4 cup chopped bacon
  • 1/4 cup chopped chives

Instructions

  1. Peel and dice the potatoes.
  2. Add to pot and fill with just enough water to submerge (This is important!).
  3. Boil - "Let the potatoes do the work!"
  4. You'll know the potatoes are ready when they are tender enough to be mashed against the side of the pot with minimal force
  5. Reduce heat to simmer. Add the butter and evaporated milk.
  6. Season to taste. I prefer Natures Seasons and garlic salt.
  7. Top with shredded cheese, chopped bacon, and chives.
